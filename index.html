Junta Commander

Tower defense games were my favorite web based games to play back in middle school and highschool, so for my second project I wanted to make one similar. I went through several canvas and tower defense tutorials and put together JUNTA COMMANDER - a tower defense game loosely based off Starship Troopers. The player must defend resource extraction teams from vicious alien bugs. More and more bugs advance on the player's position as they collect morassium, a rare mineral used to power the Earth High Junta's blockchain currency.

Ghoul Grinder

A fantasy flask game demo set in the land of Erenia, you play as a brave knight fighting off hordes of undead ghouls. This was my first group project and first full stack project. Most of the game mechanics were built with flask routing. As the lead designer for this project, I gathered, manipulated, or designed the majority of game assets, and did the CSS for all pages except the world map. I was also responsible for rendering the inventory and shop items dynamically, and creating the HP bars during combat.
